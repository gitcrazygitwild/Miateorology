Method
  • Live: scores are based on forecast vs observed weather, centered on Richmond.
  • Backfill: Open-Meteo historical comparison mode for the past year.
  • Blend: simple average of available forecast sources at each date/time.
  • Disagreement: spread between provider forecasts for temperature, precipitation, and wind.
  • Conditions: normalized daily rain, snow, thunder, and summary signals.
  • Rainbow Watch: fun estimate based on rain + clearing setup, not a scientific probability.
